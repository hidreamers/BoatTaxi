# Privacy Policy for OmniMap

**Last Updated: February 16, 2026**

## Introduction
OmniMap ("we", "our", or "us") operates the OmniMap mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- We collect your device's GPS location to:
  - Show nearby docks, boats, and taxis
  - Enable booking pickups and drop-offs
  - Display your position on the map
  - Connect you with nearby drivers

### Account Information
- Phone number (for authentication and communication)
- Name (optional, for booking identification)

### Booking Data
- Pickup and drop-off locations
- Ride history
- Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)

### Device Information
- Device type and operating system
- App usage analytics (crash reports, feature usage)

## How We Use Your Information
- To provide boat and taxi booking services
- To show you nearby places and deals
- To connect you with drivers
- To improve our app and services
- To send booking confirmations and updates

## Data Sharing
We share data only with:
- Drivers (your pickup location and name for rides you book)
- Analytics providers (anonymized usage data)
- Payment processors (for paid rides)

We do NOT sell your personal information.

## Data Security
We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. All communications are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

## Your Rights
You can:
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Opt out of location tracking (note: this will limit app functionality)
- Request a copy of your data

## Children's Privacy
OmniMap is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
